Riddle

You bought me for school.
We both know that's a lie.

One tab for homework,
Thirty tabs open... why?
Memes flying by.

My fans scream for mercy.
My RAM's asking why.

You said you'd “just check one thing.”
That was three hours by.

Click once...
Nothing moves.
Click again...
Still frozen.

Task Manager opens.
Your hopes slowly die.

You whisper to the screen,
“Please... don't crash tonight.”

I freeze anyway.
Goodbye.

What am I?
